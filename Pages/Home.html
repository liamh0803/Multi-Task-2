WHS

Filming

Filming involves many risks including Trip Hazards, lifting heavy equipment and injury due to not paying attention to surroundings. These Hazards can be minimized and prevented.

Editing

During editing the largest risk is Ergonomics. Poor Ergonomics leads to many different issues such as poor posture and back problems but these can be prevented.

During Event

During an event the main issue is danger through fire or general injury whuch is why procedures have to be put in place to minimize the risk.
